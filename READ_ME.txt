This is a simple e-commerce site with a simple CMS.

As most of my projects , i worked in "DB First" in this one as well.

Thats why i placed the Backup file of the Database inside the folder.

To make the project work , first you must attach this backup file to your Database.

Then you should change the connection string inside the web.config

And then you are good to go.
